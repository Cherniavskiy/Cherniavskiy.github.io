Hello all,

I am an artist Anna Sadovskaya. I live and work in Minsk. I cooperate with galleries producers of printed products: calendars, reproductions, postcards, etc., I take part in exhibitions. Most of the works are in private collections, and several paintings in the embassies of the Republic of Belarus in Poland, Kazakhstan, Ukraine. All the works presented here can be purchased. Also, the site contains information about exhibitions, videos about creativity, interesting materials about painting. Sincerely, Anna Sadovskaya.
